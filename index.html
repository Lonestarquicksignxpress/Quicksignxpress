⚠️ Documents a Texas Notary Public Cannot Notarize
  • Birth and Death Certificates
  • Marriage Licenses
  • Photographs
  • Documents with names that do not match the signer's ID
  • Documents with blank spaces or fields
  • Faxed or photocopied signatures
  • Backdated or forward-dated notarizations
  • Preparing or choosing documents on behalf of a signer

General Notarization

Jurats, acknowledgments, sworn statements & more

We handle a wide range of general notarial acts for individuals, families, and businesses. Whether you need a simple acknowledgment or a complex multi-document sworn statement, we ensure every notarization is performed correctly and in compliance with Texas law.

Services Include
  • Jurats — The notary certifies that the signer personally appeared and swore to the content of a document.
  • Acknowledgments — The signer appears before the notary and acknowledges signing the document voluntarily.
  • Affidavits & Sworn Statements — Written sworn declarations for legal, court, or personal use.
  • Copy Certifications — Certified true copies of eligible documents.
  • Oaths & Affirmations — Formal verbal oaths administered to witnesses or affidants.
  • Signature Witnessing — Official witnessing of a signer's signature.

Estate planning documents handled with care and confidentiality

Estate planning documents are among the most important papers you will ever sign. We treat every appointment with the discretion, professionalism, and care these sensitive documents deserve — at your home or preferred location.

  • Medical Power of Attorney — Appoint a healthcare agent to make decisions on your behalf.
  • Wills & Codicils — Notarize your last will and testament or amendments.

Remote Online Notarization (RON)

Virtual signings for clients anywhere in the world

As an authorized Texas Remote Online Notarization (RON) provider, we can notarize your documents fully online via secure video conference — no matter where you are in the United States or internationally. All you need is a device with a camera and internet access.

  • Available to clients in any U.S. state or internationally.
  • Secure, encrypted video session — completed from your phone, tablet, or computer.
  • Documents signed and notarized digitally with legally tamper-evident seals.
  • Identity verification performed using knowledge-based authentication (KBA).
  • Perfect for expats, travelers, military personnel, and out-of-state clients.
  • Same-day virtual appointments often available.

Apostille Services

We assist with the preparation and notarization of documents intended for use in foreign countries that are signatories to the Hague Convention.

  • Notarization of documents for apostille submission.
  • Guidance on the Texas apostille process through the Texas Secretary of State.
  • Commonly apostilled documents: diplomas, transcripts, powers of attorney, birth certificates, FBI background checks.
