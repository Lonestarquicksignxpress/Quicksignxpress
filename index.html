Who We Are

Your trusted notary public — ready when and where you need us.

Lone Star Quicksign Xpress Notary is a state-authorized Texas notary public service specializing in fast, professional, and convenient notarizations for individuals, families, businesses, and legal professionals.

Whether you need documents notarized at your home, office, hospital bedside, or remotely from anywhere in the world — we come to you. We offer flexible scheduling including evenings, weekends, and urgent same-day appointments.

Our mission: make notarization easy, accessible, and stress-free for every client we serve.
